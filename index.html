UN Robotics

  • When: Fridays, 6:30pm - 8:00pm
  • Where: PKI 313

UN Robotics is interested in providing students with experiences and opportunities that enhance their understanding of and skill set in robotics through participating in fun, yet challenging robotics activities.

This year, we hope to give meeting attendees more hands-on robotics experience through mini-lessons with CEENBots, while still participating in larger projects such as creating a camera-aiming device to go on a high-altitude balloon.

If you are a UNO/UNL student, and are interested in programming and learning more about robotics, then join us on MavSync and come to our meetings!

PKI - IEEE

  • When: First Thursday of each month, 5:30pm - 7:00pm (plus intermittently throughout the months)
  • Where: PKI 158 for meetings and PKI 313 and 311 for projects

PKI - IEEE is an electrical engineering club where we bring in speakers from the surrounding industry, do PCB project builds in labs, and host fun game night events. Our club is open to all majors and those looking to get a better understanding of electronics and electrical engineering.
