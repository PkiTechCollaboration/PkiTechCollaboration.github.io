PKI Tech Collaboration calendar

ACM Club

  • When: Thursdays, 7:00pm - 8:00pm
  • Where: PKI second floor classroom

ACM is a club for people interested in programming and computer science. We:

  • Attend programming competitions and hackathons (the biggest one is icpc)
  • Host talks about interesting programming/computer science/software engineering topics (past topics: git basic, introduction to the ember framework, intermediate bash programming)
  • Run the semsterly PKI programming competition and the high school CSE week programming competition
  • Occasionally host interesting speakers from software engineering industry

You can find more information by joining our google plus community.

UN Robotics

  • When: Mondays, 7:00pm - 8:00pm
  • Where: PKI 313

UN Robotics is interested in providing students with experiences and opportunities that enhance their understanding of and skill set in robotics through participating in fun, yet challenging robotics activities.

This year, we hope to give meeting attendees more hands-on robotics experience through mini-lessons with CEENBots, while still participating in larger projects such as creating a camera-aiming device to go on a high-altitude balloon.

If you are a UNO/UNL student, and are interested in programming and learning more about robotics, then join us on MavSync and come to our meetings!

PKI - IEEE

  • 1st Tuesday of evening of every month from 5:30-8:00pm
  • No fixed room -- see Facebook and MavSync for annoucements

PKI - IEEE is an Electrical and Computer Engineering club where we bring in speakers from the surrounding industry, do PCB project builds in labs, and host fun game night events. Our club is open to all majors and those looking to get a better understanding of electronics, electrical, and computer engineering.
